General Tso’s Chicken # 2

Posted on April 14, 2011 by chef

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tb Cornstarch
  • 1 lb Boned, skinned chicken thigh-or breast cut into 2 inch-chuncks
  • 4 Green onions, cut diagonally
  • 4 ts Cornstarch
  • 4 ts Sugar
  • 4 ts Rice vinegar
  • 6 tb Soy sauce-into 1 inch piecesVegetable oil
  • 16 sm Dried Chiles
  • 1 Clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 ts Grated fresh ginger
  • 1/4 c Chicken broth
  • 1/4 c Water
  • 1/4 c Dry sherry
